( Explore San Mateo, Rizal's first tourism estate in a mountain terrain offering majestic views of Metro Manila, Sierra Madre mountain ranges and Laguna de Bay.)

Timberland Highlands Resort is the new premier business and leisure destination in San Mateo Rizal providing a unique guest experience in a mountain eco-development. The property is situated within Timberland Heights, an exclusive suburban townscape at 250-450 meters above sea level offering breathtaking views at every turn. The community went through delicate and extensive planning to ensure that the ecosystem is preserved for everyone to have a holistic experience with nature.
